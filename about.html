Wanting to give more children the chance to a tuition-free education, Bro. Eli Soriano, together with Bro. Daniel Razon and the Members Church of God International, founded the La Verdad Christian School. Giving free education to students from pre-school to college, the first La Verdad Christian School was founded in Apalit, Pampanga. Later on, another branch was established in Muñoz, Manila, to cater to scholars in that area.

HUMBLE BEGINNINGS..

LVCS first open its doors at Mc Arthur Highway, Sampaloc, Apalit, Pampanga in 1999 through the effort of Bro. Eliseo F. Soriano who serves as the Chairman Emeritus and Dr. Daniel S. Razon as the President of the school.

It was officially registered in the Securities and Exchange Commission last May 27, 1998 offering Pre-school level and Elementary grades. 












It started its first year of operation last School Year 1999-2000 with Government Permit No. E-037 for Elementary Level with 50 students and Government Recognition No. E-026 s. 2000 for Pre-School Level with just 13 students. The school has just 63 total number of students back then.

FIRST HIGHSCHOOL GRADUATING STUDENTS 


One year after its operation, La Verdad Christian School decided to offer Secondary Level with Government Permit No. S-027, s. 2001. It started with 39 high school students, 30 from first year level and only 9 second year students. It was 2002 when the school had its first batch of graduating students from high school department.

FIRST TERTIARY COURSES

From a small and simple institutions, La Verdad Christian School expands its horizon by adding tertiary level under the banner of La Verdad Christian College ( LVCC ) with TESDA programs offering the following courses: 2 years Mass Communication Technology, 2 Years Nursing Assistant, 2 years Computer Programming, 2 years Office Management, 1 year International Cookery and 6 months Heavy Equipment Operation. It started with only 154 college students. 






FIRST DEGREE COURSE

Being committed to quality and relevant education, LVCC decided to offer degree courses under the Commission on Higher Education. It was AY 2008-2009 when the institution received its Government Permit No. HET -069, s. 2007 for the degree Bachelor of Arts in Broadcasting with Dr. Daniel S. Razon as the Dean. It further receives its Government Recognition No. GR-064 last 2010.

STUDY NOW, PAY NEVER!


In God’s will, through the initiation of its Founding Chairman and President to help deserving but poor students, LVCS and LVCC formally started the FULL SCHOLARSHIP GRANT to all students who doesn’t have the chance to continue their studies because of their status in life.

This scholarship grants offer FREE Tuition Fee, FREE Miscellaneous Fee, FREE Books and Manuals, FREE School and PE Uniform and FREE Lunch.
