ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS


General Requirement
    A. For Freshmen Students
          ● The original copy of the applicant’s high school diploma presented upon                enrollment; a photocopy shall be authenticated by the registrar and kept                   in the school files.
          ● The original copy of FORM 138 or report card shall be surrendered to the  
              registrar upon enrollment and shall be kept in the school files.
          ● Photocopy of birth certificate
          ● Passing of the LVCC entrance examination
          ● Four (4) copies of 2x2 size recent photo
          ● Six (6) copies of 1x1 size recent photo
          ● PEP Result / NCAE Result
          ● Good Moral Character
          ● Form 137

     B. For Transferee Students
          ● Photocopy of birth certificate
          ● Passing of the LVCC entrance examination
          ● Four (4) copies of 2x2 size recent photo
          ● Six (6) copies of 1x1 size recent photo
          ● Honorable Dismissal / Transfer Credential
          ● Official Transcript of Records
          ● Good Moral Character
